Scrum Via Trello

Running Scrum for Atomix

Scrum was a fundamental breakthrough our team experienced in our developmental efforts.

Prior to using Trello, we had a groupchat where I would assign tasks to the developers and
would work through bugs/testing all in one.

Eventually the volume of projects we worked on along with the complexity of systems and bugs required a smoother system.

Fortunately, my developers were able to point us in the right direction - that's where Scrum began!
