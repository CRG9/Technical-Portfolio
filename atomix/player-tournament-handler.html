Player Tournament Handler

Product Manager & QA: Chris Galetta

Development Work: Atomix Network Team

xTournament Summary

  1. This plugin links 9 depends to enable players to enter player vs. player tournaments on the server.
  2. The tournament handler accounts for dozens of server-side factors to ensure player stats and other player data is conserved upon entering the temporary tournament gamestate.
  3. These tournaments went on to serve players opportunities to win powerful rewards to aid them on their journey through the overarching MMO.

Below is the plugin's configuration, showcasing all of its core features.

Bracket Handler Code

To ensure sound, error free bracket handling, I led our team in manual QA to hunt out bugs and account for all possible scenarios that would break the bracket.

    Some examples included:

    - A player leaving the server while in the tournament.
    - A player being banned from the server while in the tournament.
    - A player disconnecting then rejoining the server while in a tournament.

Bracket Chart

Tournaments in this build are single elimination, offering team sizes of 1v1's, 2v2's, 3v3's, and 4v4's, with bracket sizes of up to 128 teams.
