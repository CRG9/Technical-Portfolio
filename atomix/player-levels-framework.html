Player Ranking Frameworks

Levels Framework

One opportunity players on Atomix had was the ability to acquire resources, upgrade their mining gear, and gain access to new mines on the server.

Levels were climbed by mining blocks and earning progress until reaching ever increasing progress requirements.

In doing so, players gained access to new powerups and upgrades that helped them climb levels faster and become a true competitor on the server.

Level Cost Calculations

Due to the importance of maintaining a smooth feel for progression through the game,
our level up curve was comprised of different slopes that made up the greater curve.

Creating this curve-comprised-of-curves turned out to be effective at accomplishing our desired end result!

Level Cost Curves Configuration

Seen below are the final calculations that made up our level progresion curve.

You'll notice that the first few levels are not dictated by a calculation, where as the latter are
defined explicitly by a formula.

This is by design! The early levels are meant to hook the player, where as the latter levels are meant to provide the challenge.

Level Up Actions Configuration

After a player gained enough progress, a level up action automatically occured.

Each level gave access to currencies, unlocks, and new abilities that propelled the player forward in the game.
