Web Based Leaderboards

Breeding Competition on Atomix

  1. By nature, Atomix is a competitive server with a heavy emphasis placed on player achievement and rivalry.
  2. The purpose of the web leaderboards was to further emphasize that competition and encourage players to get on their grind and climb the rankings.
  3. Through the collaboration with a freelancing web developer, we successfully developed a variety of leaderboards that encompassed 10 stats that players could compete on.
  4. These leaderboards successfully broke down stats into hourly, daily, weekly, and monthly timeframes by linking to our leaderboards software and utilizing an API.
