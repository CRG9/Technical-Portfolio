About Monte Carlo Simulator

What is a Monte Carlo Simulator?

This simulator is a critical decision-support tool for our trading team.

It stress-tests strategies against thousands of market scenarios, quantifying risk and revealing the true probability of profits or losses before real capital is ever deployed.

How It Works:

  1. Takes in Trade Parameters
  2. Runs [x] Simulations Based on Parameters
  3. Uses Win Rate and Pure Randomization to Determine Wins
  4. Outputs Range Based Results for Analysis
  5. Offers "Drill Down" for Dissecting Result Ranges
